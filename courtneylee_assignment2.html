Lee Island

Welcome to Lee Island, an oasis of sophisticated elegance. Whether you are honeymooners or just in need of a relaxing vacation, Lee Island offers the most beautiful white sand beaches in the world. Lee Island is home to the most exclusive invite only hotel, with a guest list so top secret not even the Queen of England herself has been. The 20 sq km Island offers only one hotel, allowing the guests to have the utmost peace and quiet on their vacation.
